Our Story: From a Passion for Takoyaki to Your Plate

The story of TakoYum PH began with a simple, yet powerful love for food — and a deep appreciation for the art of Japanese cuisine. Our founder, a lifelong Takoyaki enthusiast, traveled to Japan and discovered the unforgettable joy of biting into a perfectly crispy, tender Takoyaki ball. That moment sparked something special: a dream to bring that same authentic flavor and experience back home to the Philippines.

TakoYum PH was established in 2023 following years of refining recipes and locating the best ingredients. We were aware that our goal was to introduce a bit of Japan's street food culture into the center of our neighborhood, not just a takoyaki stand. We meticulously prepared each batch with an emphasis on quality, genuineness, and creativity, ensuring that each bite carried the same enchantment that motivated our journey.

From our humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a beloved local spot, continuously experimenting with flavors, toppings, and fillings, while staying true to our roots. Today, TakoYum PH is proud to be a trusted name for high-quality Takoyaki, serving up deliciousness with every order. Whether you're here for a quick snack or sharing a meal with friends, we promise to bring you the best of Japan, one Takoyaki ball at a time.
